Worry Dolls

Has your stress ever been too much to handle? Worry not, Worry Dolls to the rescue! Worry dolls is an app that allows you to write about your problems to your dolls. Instead of carrying this heavy load on your shoulders alone, your dolls will help carry it with you. This app allows you to directly transfer your stressful thoughts and problems to your dolls. Your dolls will worry about your problems so you don’t have to.


Replika AI

Ever want someone to talk to? Replika AI is a source that allows you to create an avatar designated to be your friend. Your Replika can listen to your problems, give you advice, make casual conversations, joke around, and more! No need to worry about late messages, your avatar will respond right away!


Headspace

Headspace is an app that focuses on a variety of aspects pertaining to your mental health. This app helps people with their anxiety, their sleep schedules, their stress through meditation and clearing one’s head. If you’re looking for some peace within yourself, this app will surely be the right fit for you.


Circles

If you’re looking so find a group of people to share your problems with, Circles is the perfect app for you. Circles allows you to meet a group of people and communicate with them about your thoughts. People often find that having someone to share their problems with bring a sense of comfort and compassion for themselves and for others. Circles is a form of group therapy and this site offers meetings to talk about your communications with people who struggle with the same problems.


BetterHelp

BetterHelp is a website that offers therapy virtually. This site not only offers individual therapy but also couples therapy if needed. This website allows you to take a questionnaire to see what kind of therapist you are looking for in order to make you most comfortable. BetterHelp offers professional help to those who are looking to talking about their depression, trauma, anxiety, and more. These therapists will provide you with what you’re looking for and expecting to get out of their help.


Wysa

Wysa is a site that offers AI support therapy. This site allows you to be completely anonymous and it is also guided with a professional. The AI will provide advice to your problems and support for your mental health 24/7. If you’re looking to talk about your struggles anonymously, using this source would be beneficial as well as helpful for you and your mind.


Youper

Are you looking for something professional yet virtual? Youper provides you video visits with your doctors, prescribes necessary medication, and therapy for those struggling with depression, anxiety, and more. This site connects you with professional therapists and offers your results and improvement after meetings with your therapists.


988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

If you’re struglling with depression and/or expereiancing sucidial thoughts, please don’t hesitate to call the Suicide Hotline. This site offers help on on live chats and messages as well as phone calls. This number gives you someone to listen to and you can call them at anytime. Being depressed or suicidal is nothing to be ashamed of and it is always improtant to ask for help. You’re never alone, and you’re never weak for asking for help.
